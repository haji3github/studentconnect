    A place for students to connect, collaborate, meet and greet each other in an efficient way.

    Student Connect web app aims to bridge the gap between students who are looking to connect, share resource and collaborate on academic projects. Save your time by finding all you need at one place!

    We are final year students pursuing Bachelors of Technology in the field of Computer Science and Engineering

    We are a team of three members from Shadan Womens college of engineering and technology.

    We have developed this website for students who are struggling swtiching through various apps and their memory space being filled up with large documents.

    • We have upload and browse page where resources can be uploaded and downloaded at any time and place.
    • Call and Chat funtionality which allows students to connect and have a virtual meeting.
    • The post page allows to post updates of your studies so that it can be motivating for your peers too.

    Hence, we are looking forward to seeing students around us enjoy the benefits of using this web app.
